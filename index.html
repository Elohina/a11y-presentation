Ireland

Central Statistics Office

There were a total of 643,131 people who stated they had a disability in April 2016, accounting for 13.5 per cent of the population.

Key facts

  • 8% of children are disabled
  • 18% of working age adults are disabled
  • 44% of pension age adults are disabled
